### **What are node-based tools?**

A **node** is a visual building block that performs a single operationâ€”like "generate image," "apply style transfer," or "upscale."

You connect nodes together to create **workflows**, where the output of one step becomes the input for the next.
***
### **Why are they perfect for AI?**
- ğŸ”— **Chain Bite-Sized Tasks**: Build up to final assets by connecting small, focused operations together.
- **ğŸ”¬ Compare & Test**: Easily test many model/prompt combinations to see which you like best, then narrow down from there.
