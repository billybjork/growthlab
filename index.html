About this tool

This is a Markdown-based slide viewer designed for live sessions and self-directed learning. All content is stored as plain text Markdown files in the sessions/ folder, making it easy to version control, edit, and share.

  • For instructors: Edit Markdown, refresh the browser, and present updated decks during live sessions.
  • For students: Review sessions at your own pace or download the source materials.
